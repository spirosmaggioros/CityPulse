## 🌍 The Problem

Every city has issues — broken streetlights, potholes, power outages, illegal dumping, traffic hazards. Reporting them usually means navigating confusing government websites, filling out lengthy forms, or making phone calls that go nowhere.

**Most issues go unreported. The ones that do get reported are often duplicated, misclassified, or sent to the wrong department.**

---

## 💡 The Solution

CityPulse changes everything. Just **open a chat and describe what's happening** — in your own words, in plain language. Our AI handles the rest:

- Pinpoints your exact location
- Understands and categorizes the problem
- Checks if someone nearby already reported the same thing
- Routes your report to the right municipal department
- Keeps you informed every step of the way

**No forms. No dropdowns. No frustration.** Just a conversation.

## ✨ What CityPulse Does

### 🗣️ Report Issues by Simply Talking

Describe your problem the way you'd tell a friend. CityPulse understands natural language and guides you through the reporting process conversationally. Whether it's *"the streetlight outside my house has been off for a week"* or *"there's a massive pothole at Athen's National Garden"* — we get it.

### 📍 Automatic Location Detection

Just mention your street, neighborhood, or nearby landmark. CityPulse automatically converts your description into a precise location on the map — no need to drop pins or type coordinates. Even vague descriptions like *"near the park on Vassilissis Sofias"* are enough.

### 🏷️ Smart Issue Classification

CityPulse automatically determines:
- **Which department** should handle your report (Municipality, Police, Fire Department, Environmental Services, Transportation, or Health)
- **How urgent** the issue is
- **A clear title and description** for your report — even if your original message was informal

### 🔍 Duplicate Detection

Before submitting your report, CityPulse checks if someone in your area has already reported the same issue. If they have, your voice is added to the existing report — making it more visible and more likely to be resolved. No wasted effort, no cluttered databases.

### 📸 Attach Photos

See something wrong? Snap a photo and share it directly in the chat. Visual evidence helps departments respond faster and understand the issue at a glance.

### 🌐 Real-Time Information

Need to know if emergency services are already aware of a situation? CityPulse can search for real-time information about ongoing incidents, service disruptions, and emergency responses in your area.

### 💬 Quick Actions

Don't know where to start? CityPulse offers one-click shortcuts for common queries:
- **"Explain crime rates in my area"** — Get insights about safety in your neighborhood
- **"What's the most urgent issue right now?"** — See what needs immediate attention
- **"What's the most upvoted issue?"** — Discover what your community cares about most

### 🔄 Seamless Conversations

CityPulse remembers your entire conversation. You can report your location first, describe the problem later, ask questions in between — and everything stays connected. No need to repeat yourself.

### 🧠 Contextual Guidance

Not sure if your issue is worth reporting? CityPulse can explain the significance of different urban problems, help you understand what qualifies as urgent, and guide you through the process with helpful context.

The entire process takes **under 2 minutes** — most of which is just you typing naturally.

No. Just open CityPulse and start chatting.

**Q: What if I don't know the exact address?**

CityPulse will detect it and add your voice to the existing report — increasing its visibility and priority.

Yes. Your conversations are processed securely and are not shared with third parties.
